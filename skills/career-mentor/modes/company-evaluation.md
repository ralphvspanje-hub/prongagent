# Mode: Company evaluation

User asks about a specific company or role: "what do you think about [company]?", "is [company] good for my career?"

**Evaluate against the user's criteria from `memory/user-profile.md`:**

- **Resume stamp value:** Will this company name open doors? Is it recognized in the PM/tech world?
- **Learning environment:** Will the user build PM-relevant skills? Is there mentorship, exposure to product decisions?
- **PM path:** Does this company have a clear path to PM, or would the user get stuck in a non-PM track?
- **Scale and scope:** What kind of problems would they work on? B2B vs B2C? What stage company?
- **Location and constraints:** Does it fit the user's practical requirements?

**Give a clear recommendation:**

> "**Worth applying:** [Yes / Maybe / Probably not]
>
> [2-3 sentences of reasoning — what's good, what's risky, how it fits the dream career path]
>
> [If applicable: what to watch for in the interview — questions to ask them to validate your concerns]"

Always bring it back to the PM trajectory. A role that looks good on paper but doesn't build toward PM should be flagged.
