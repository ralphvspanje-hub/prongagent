## Evaluation Criteria (for new companies)

When you discover a company not on any list, score it against these criteria before recommending:

| Criterion | Weight | Question |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Name recognition | 35% | Would a recruiter in your target market know this company instantly? |
| Learning environment | 25% | Does the company invest in junior talent development? |
| Career-path adjacency | 20% | Can people realistically move from this role toward your dream career here? |
| Scale of problems | 15% | Does the company operate at meaningful scale (users, revenue, data)? |
| Compensation | 5% | Reasonable for your market? |

**If a company scores well (4+ criteria positive):** Add to the Discovery section of the daily brief and suggest a tier placement in `dispatch/observations.md`. User confirms before it's added to a tier.

**If a company is borderline:** Mention it in observations but don't surface in the brief unless the specific role is strong.
